Instructions

Choices

In this final section, you will be asked to make a series of similar decisions. Each line is a separate decision.

Each decision consists of allocating a total of 50 tokens between an early date and a later date.

For each decision you get a new set of 50 tokens.

Tokens allocated to the later date are worth 20 cents each. Tokens allocated to the early date are worth an amount that changes from line to line.

The program does the multiplication for you, showing the totals for each date based on your current choices.

There are 9 tabs with 5 lines on each tab.

Each line has a different value for the early date.

Each tab has a different pair of dates.

The early date and the late date are shown on the calendar at the top.

You can navigate back and forth between the tabs. When you are finished, click the button at the bottom to submit all of your decisions.

You must make a choice in all decisions before you can submit.


Payment

After you submit, one of your 45 lines will be chosen at random. You will be paid based on your choices for this line. You will also receive an extra $5 thank you on each of the two dates.

Example:

If the line selected at random was:

Early Date: November 29th, tokens worth 16 cents

Late Date: January 17th, tokens worth 20 cents

And you put 20 tokens in the early date and 30 tokens in the late date, then you would receiv

Early Date: $5 thank you + 20 tokens * 16 cents/token = $8.20 on November 29th

Late Date: $5 thank you + 30 tokens * 20 cents/token = $11 on January 17th
